Steps

  1. Take as big pot as you can find.
  2. Pour all the liquid ingredients into it. Boil it.
  3. Place the remaining ingredients and stew under the lid until ready.
  4. At the end you can add some spices, but it will not help.
  5. DONE! Congratulations, you can look in the pot and see there the mirror of your soul!
